# Velia — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 2026-06-29**

Velia ("the app", "we") is a privacy‑first menstrual‑health tracker. This policy explains what data the app handles and how. The short version: **your health data stays on your device. We don't run a server, we don't collect it, and we don't sell or share it.**

## Who we are
Velia is published by **[YOUR LEGAL NAME / COMPANY]**. Contact: **[support@velia.app]**.

## What data the app handles — and where it lives
Everything you enter is stored **locally on your device, encrypted at rest**. We do not operate servers that receive your data, and the app makes no network calls to us.

- **Cycle & health data** — period days, flow, symptoms, mood, energy, sleep, pain, notes, and (in "trying to conceive" mode) basal body temperature, cervical mucus, LH‑test and intimacy logs.
- **Profile inputs** — average cycle length, period length, reproductive‑status group, optional birth year.
- **Account (optional)** — if you create an account, your email and a **salted, hashed** representation of your password (PBKDF2). Your password itself is never stored. Using an account is optional; you can use Velia without one.
- **App settings** — language, reminders, app‑icon and lock preferences.

We do **not** collect device identifiers, location, contacts, advertising IDs, or analytics.

## How your data is protected
- Stored in an **encrypted** on‑device store; the encryption key is held in the device **Keychain**.
- Optional **Face ID / Touch ID / passcode** app lock and per‑profile **PIN**.
- The app screen is hidden in the iOS app switcher.

## Third parties
- **We use no third‑party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.**
- **Apple / App Store:** purchases (the optional subscription) are processed by Apple. We never see your payment details. Apple's handling of purchases is governed by Apple's privacy policy. We receive only Apple's purchase/entitlement status to unlock features.

## Notifications
Reminders (upcoming period, daily logging, fertile window) are **local notifications scheduled on your device**. Their content is generated on‑device and not sent to any server.

## Your choices and rights
- **Use without an account**, and **delete your account in‑app** at any time (Settings → Account → Delete Account).
- **Delete all data** by removing the app, which erases its on‑device storage.
- Because we hold no copy of your data on a server, requests to access/export/erase are satisfied directly on your device.

## Children
Velia is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your region) and we do not knowingly collect their data.

## Medical disclaimer
Velia provides estimates and tracking for general wellness. It is **not** a contraceptive, fertility‑treatment, or diagnostic tool, and is not a substitute for professional medical advice.
